    HISTORICAL Places

    In The World!!

    Embark on a journey through time and explore the captivating wonders of the world's most amazing historic places. Uncover the rich tapestry of human history, from ancient civilizations to architectural marvels, each site tells a unique story waiting to be discovered..



    Colosseum, Italy

    We were spoiled when we first visited the Colosseum of Rome staying in a luxury apartment
    looking directly at the Colosseum every morning from our bedroom window. It is an extraordinary
    sight to wake up to and people agree. The Colosseum is one of the most popular tourist
    destinations on Earth.

    Chichen Itza, Mexico

    Chichen Itza was the first historical place we ever visited. Located in the Yucátan Peninsula,
    this must-see archaeological site was named one of the new Seven Wonders of the World.
    This UNESCO World Heritage Site blew my mind.

    Khai Dinh Tomb, Hue

    Khai Dinh Tomb, officially known as the Ứng Mausoleum, is situated in Chau Chu mountain,
    about 10km away from Hue city, Vietnam. The tomb is the smallest, but most costly and recent
    one in comparison with other tombs of Nguyen royals
